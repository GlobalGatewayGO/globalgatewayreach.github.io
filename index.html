What we do

One partner for the whole journey from supplier to shipment.

Most importers lose money in the gaps — the wrong supplier, the unchecked order, the shipment that stalls at customs. We close those gaps end to end.

Supplier sourcing

Tell us the product. We identify, vet, and negotiate with factories directly in Chinese, then hand you a shortlist with real prices — not marketplace markups.

Samples & negotiation

We collect samples, compare quality, and push for better terms, MOQs, and lead times before a single dollar goes into production.

Quality inspection

Before anything ships, we inspect the goods against your spec — so defects are caught in the factory, not in your warehouse.

Shipping & logistics

Sea, air, or express. We arrange freight, handle export documents, and track the container until it clears customs in your country.

Private label & OEM

Want it under your own brand? We coordinate custom packaging, logos, and OEM production so the product arrives ready to sell.

Ongoing supply

Once we find a winner, we keep reordering, monitoring quality, and managing the relationship so your supply line stays steady.

On the ground in China

More than sourcing — we host you in China.

Some things you can't do over email. When you want to see the factory yourself, build relationships in person, or grow your name in China, we plan it around you. Every tour and trip is built to your goals, not pulled off a shelf.

Factory tours

We arrange and prepare the whole visit — shortlist the factories, book the schedule, and take you there in person to see the production line before you commit.

Translation & interpreting

A native Chinese-speaking team travels with you. Meetings, negotiations, contracts, and dinners — nothing gets lost in translation.

Personal brand in China

We help you build and protect your personal brand on the ground — introductions, presence, and guarding your name and reputation in the Chinese market.

Business travel, your way

Travel with us for work, then unwind — we fold real rest time and curated experiences into the trip so business in China isn't only meetings.

Private & group travel

Not here for business? We design trips for private travellers and companies alike — a fully custom China itinerary, planned and guided by people who live here.

Built around you

There's no fixed package. We shape every tour and trip to your needs, pace, and budget — tell us what you want and we build it.
