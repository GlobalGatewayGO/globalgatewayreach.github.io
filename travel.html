Where we host you

Anywhere in China — but here's where we travel most.

We work across the country. These are the regions where we run the most trips because the work and the experience both make sense there.

Guangdong · Shenzhen

Electronics and modern China

Factories for electronics, consumer goods, and accessories. Pair the sourcing with a city that runs on speed — design districts, food, and the country's tech edge.

Zhejiang · Yiwu

The world's wholesale market

If you're sourcing anything from gifts to household goods, this is where it begins. We guide you through the markets and connect you to the factories behind them.

Shanghai & the Yangtze

Business, brands, and culture

The natural base for premium business travel. Meetings during the week, weekends in Suzhou, Hangzhou, or the water towns — the polished side of working in China.

Beijing & the north

Decisions and history

When the trip involves government, large enterprises, or the cultural heart of the country. We arrange the meetings and pair them with the parts of China you came to see.

Guangxi · Yunnan

Off the standard route

For travellers who want China outside the obvious cities — landscapes, minority cultures, food. Slower trips, planned with local guides.

Hainan · the south

Where we're based

Our home base. Beaches, free-trade zone, slower pace — ideal as the recovery leg after an intensive sourcing or business trip.
